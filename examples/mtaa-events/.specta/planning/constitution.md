# Constitution

## Principles

- Design every visitor-facing experience for small screens first, then enhance it for larger screens without reducing clarity or usability.
- Make event discovery fast and understandable by presenting consistent event information and useful category and location filters.
- Keep content access independent of its storage source so that replacing JSON with Firestore does not require redesigning visitor-facing behavior.
- Treat event browsing as public and read-only in the initial product; authentication and event-management capabilities remain outside the current scope.
- Provide event details that are accurate, legible, and sufficient for a visitor to decide whether and how to attend.
- Prefer accessible, resilient interactions that remain usable across common mobile devices and connection conditions.
