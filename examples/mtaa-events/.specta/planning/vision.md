# Vision

## Mtaa Events: Local Community Event Discovery

## Problem

People lack a simple, mobile-friendly way to find relevant events happening in their local communities. Event information can be difficult to browse by interest or place, making nearby activities less visible and reducing community participation.

## Audience

Residents and visitors who want to discover local community events from a mobile device, especially people looking for activities that match a preferred category or location.

## Outcome

Visitors can quickly browse a directory of local events, narrow results by category and location, and view the details needed to decide whether to attend, while the event content remains portable enough to move from an initial JSON source to Firestore later.
