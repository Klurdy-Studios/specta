# Constitution

## Principles

- Keep complaint workflows simple and focused on capture, retrieval, status tracking, and resolution.
- Treat the JSON data store as the authoritative record and preserve valid, consistent complaint data on every change.
- Retrieve and mutate complaint information through server-side actions so data access rules remain centralized.
- Protect customer and complaint information by exposing only the data required for each user interaction.
- Make complaint states and outcomes clear enough that staff can understand what requires attention and what has been resolved.
- Prefer straightforward, maintainable solutions appropriate to a small file-backed application over unnecessary complexity.
