# Vision

## Help Dock Complaint Management

## Problem

Customer complaints can be lost, handled inconsistently, or difficult to retrieve when there is no simple central record of each issue and its progress. A lightweight complaint management system is needed to capture complaints, preserve their current status and relevant details, and make them easy for customer care staff to find and follow through to resolution.

## Audience

Customer care staff who record, review, update, and resolve customer complaints, and the customers whose concerns need accountable handling.

## Outcome

Customer care staff can reliably record and retrieve complaints, understand their current status, and manage them through resolution using a simple application backed by JSON data and server-side information access.
